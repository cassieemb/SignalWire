SignalWire Messaging Info

Customer Data

The customer data center will allow you to upload customer lists, review existing customer lists, and review opted out users. If you try to upload a previously opted out customer, you can remove them from the list you're uploading and proceed.


Upload and review Customer Data.

Number Groups

By using number groups you can put your account numbers in a pool and use it to send your text blasts out. Sticky sender ensures that customers receive a message from the same number every time!


Create and view number groups.

Shortened URL Generator

This section will allow you to generate shortened links from full URLs and view information about your existing shortened URLs including when they were last used.


Generate shortened URLs.

Text Blasts

This bulk messaging hub will allow you to choose from uploaded customer lists, choose from numbers on your account or send from number groups, and easily customize your messaging


Send a text blast.

Error Center

Stay on top of issues with the error center - view all failed messages that used the status callback feature or pull a report of failed/undelivered messages within any date range.


View recent error history.

Message History

You can view all message history over the last 24 hours, or apply new filters to view message history by date range, from number, or to number.


View message history.
