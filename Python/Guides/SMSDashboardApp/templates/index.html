SignalWire Messaging Info

Customer Data

The customer data center will allow you to upload customer lists, review existing lists, and review opted out user lists.

Number Groups

Create number groups to send to your customers from. Sticky sender ensures that customers receive a message from the same number every time!

Shortened URL Generator

This section will allow you to generate shortened links from full URLs.

Text Blasts

This bulk messaging hub will allow you to choose from uploaded customer lists, choose from numbers on your account or send from number groups, and easily customize your messaging

Error Center

You can easily view error history on all your messaging for the past 7 days here - click a message to get additional details!

Message History

You can view all message history over the last 24 hours, or apply new filters to view message history by date range, from number, or to number. Click a message to get additional details!
